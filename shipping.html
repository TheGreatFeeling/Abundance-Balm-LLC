Shipping

Please review your shipping address carefully before completing your purchase.

Free Shipping

Standard shipping is included with your purchase of Abundance Balm unless otherwise stated during checkout.

Expedited shipping may not be available for every order.

Processing Times

Because Abundance Balm is handcrafted in small batches, processing times may vary depending on order volume.

We work to prepare and ship every order as quickly as possible. Your estimated shipping and delivery dates will appear in your Etsy order details.

No Returns or Exchanges

Due to the personal-care nature of Abundance Balm and for health and hygiene reasons, all sales are final.

We do not accept returns or exchanges, including unopened products, except where required by applicable law.

Please review the ingredient list, safety information, product size, and order details before completing your purchase.

Damaged, Defective, or Incorrect Orders

If your order arrives damaged, defective, or different from what you ordered, please contact us within 7 days of delivery.

Include your order number, a description of the issue, and clear photographs of the product and packaging.

After reviewing the information, we will work with you to provide an appropriate resolution.

Lost or Delayed Packages

Once an order has been transferred to the shipping carrier, delivery timing may be affected by weather, carrier delays, holidays, or other circumstances outside our control.

If tracking shows an unusual delay, please contact us and we will help you review the available shipping information.

Incorrect Shipping Address

Customers are responsible for providing a complete and accurate shipping address.

If you notice an error after placing your order, contact us immediately. We cannot guarantee that an address can be changed after an order has entered processing or has shipped.

Additional shipping charges may apply if an order must be resent because an incorrect or incomplete address was provided.
