The Story Behind Abundance Balm

Years ago, I was at Nelson Ledges enjoying one of my favorite places on earth. It was one of those brutally hot, humid summer days. Between the heat, humidity, and hours of walking, I started chafing badly and was becoming pretty miserable.

Out of nowhere, a kind hippie girl noticed I was struggling. Without hesitation, she walked over and handed me a small metal tin labeled Abundance Balm.

I gave it a try.

The relief was almost immediate.

To this day, I have never found another product that works as well for relieving the discomfort of chafing. That tiny tin turned what had become a miserable day into a great one, and I never forgot it.

Years passed, but I kept that little tin. Her name and the ingredients were printed on the label, and I hoped that one day I could thank her for what she had done.

I searched social media, searched online, and followed every lead I could think of, hoping to find her.

Unfortunately, I never did.

Using the ingredients listed on the original tin, I carefully recreated the balm that had helped me all those years ago.

As a tribute to the woman whose simple act of kindness inspired everything that followed, I kept the original style of the label and added these words:

Inspired by Alexandria Sacketka

The original tin has been through years of adventures. The label is faded and worn, so I can't make out her last name with complete certainty, but I wanted to honor her and the kindness she showed me.

Today, every tin of Abundance Balm is made with that same spirit in mind.

I personally use it for:

  • Chafing
  • Dry lips
  • A light natural fragrance
  • Minor itch relief from bug bites

For me, Abundance Balm has never been just another skincare product.

It is a reminder that one small act of kindness can stay with someone for a lifetime.

My hope is that every tin carries that same spirit forward.


Kindness has a way of coming full circle.
