WHAT'S INSIDE

Simple Ingredients.
Thoughtfully Chosen.

Every ingredient in Abundance Balm was selected to help create a rich, smooth balm inspired by the original formula that started it all.

🥥 Coconut Oil

Forms the rich, smooth base of the balm and helps it glide comfortably onto the skin.

🌰 Sweet Almond Oil

A lightweight botanical oil that adds softness and helps create a silky feel.

🌿 Jojoba Oil

Adds balance to the blend while providing a smooth, non-greasy finish.

🍊 Orange Essential Oil

Provides a naturally bright citrus aroma that lifts the overall fragrance.

🌲 Pine Essential Oil

Adds a fresh woodland scent inspired by nature.

🌱 Mint Essential Oil

Contributes a refreshing sensation and crisp herbal aroma.

🪵 Cinnamon Essential Oil

Completes the blend with warm, comforting notes that round out the fragrance.

Made in Small Batches

Every tin of Abundance Balm is handcrafted in small batches with care and attention to quality. We believe great products don't need dozens of complicated ingredients—just thoughtfully chosen botanicals and careful craftsmanship.
