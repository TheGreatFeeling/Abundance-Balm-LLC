Important

For external cosmetic use only. Abundance Balm is a moisturizer and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent eczema, psoriasis, pain, inflammation, infection, or any other disease or medical condition.

01 · Intended use

What this product is designed for

Abundance Balm is intended to moisturize intact external body skin and provide a rich botanical aroma and conditioning feel.

It is not a medication, wound treatment, burn treatment, pain reliever, eczema treatment, or substitute for professional medical care.

02 · Do not use

Do not apply Abundance Balm to:

Eczema or active skin conditions

Do not apply to eczema, psoriasis, dermatitis, unexplained rashes, hives, or other active skin conditions unless a qualified healthcare professional has approved its use.

Broken or damaged skin

Do not apply to cuts, punctures, scrapes, open wounds, blisters, cracked skin, bleeding skin, or recently shaved skin.

Burned or inflamed skin

Do not apply to sunburns, heat burns, chemical burns, inflamed skin, swollen areas, or skin that feels unusually hot.

Sensitive areas

Avoid the eyes, eyelids, lips, inside the nose, mouth, genitals, mucous membranes, and other sensitive areas.

Before sun or heat exposure

Do not apply immediately before tanning, prolonged direct sun exposure, saunas, heating pads, or other intense heat.

Known allergies

Do not use if you are allergic or sensitive to coconut, almonds or tree nuts, jojoba, citrus, pine, mint, cinnamon, fragrance ingredients, or any listed ingredient.

03 · Patch testing

Patch test before regular use

  1. Apply a very small amount to a clean, dry area of intact skin on the inner arm.
  2. Do not cover the area tightly, apply heat, or add another skincare product over it.
  3. Wait at least 24 hours and observe the area.
  4. Do not continue using the product if redness, itching, burning, swelling, pain, blistering, or another unwanted reaction occurs.

A successful patch test does not guarantee that irritation or an allergic reaction cannot occur later.

04 · Directions

How to use

  1. Use only on clean, dry, intact external body skin.
  2. Begin with a fingertip-sized amount. This is a concentrated product, so use sparingly.
  3. Warm the balm gently between your fingertips or palms.
  4. Massage a thin layer into the desired area using gentle motions.
  5. Wash your hands after application.

Do not swallow

Keep the product away from food, beverages, children, and pets. The product is not edible.

05 · If a reaction occurs

Stop use immediately

Stop using the product and gently wash the affected area if you experience burning, stinging, redness, itching, swelling, blistering, worsening dryness, pain, or another unexpected reaction.

Contact a healthcare professional for medical advice. Seek urgent medical care for trouble breathing, facial or throat swelling, faintness, widespread hives, severe blistering, or another serious reaction.

Keep the product tin, ingredient information, purchase details, and batch or lot number available when reporting a reaction.

06 · Ingredient information

Current formula ingredients

Coconut oil, sweet almond oil, jojoba oil, orange oil, pine oil, mint oil, and cinnamon oil.

This website description must be updated whenever the formula or supplier ingredient declarations change. The final retail label should use the legally appropriate ingredient names and order.

07 · Additional precautions

Speak with a professional before use when appropriate

  • During pregnancy or while nursing
  • Before use on children
  • If you have highly sensitive skin
  • If you have a diagnosed skin condition
  • If you have a history of severe allergies
  • If you take medication that affects the skin
