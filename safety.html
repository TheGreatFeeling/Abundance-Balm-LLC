USE WITH CARE

Safety & Use

A little care goes a long way. Please read these guidelines before using Abundance Balm.

For External Use Only

Abundance Balm is intended for external cosmetic use only.

Do not swallow or apply inside the mouth, nose, eyes, ears, or other sensitive areas.

Patch Test Before Use

Before using the balm more broadly, apply a small amount to a limited area of clean, dry skin.

Wait at least 24 hours and discontinue use if redness, burning, itching, swelling, rash, or other irritation develops.

Avoid Broken or Irritated Skin

Do not apply the balm to open wounds, severely irritated skin, active rashes, or infected areas.

Do not use immediately after shaving if the skin is unusually sensitive or irritated.

Contains Nut-Derived Ingredients

Abundance Balm contains sweet almond oil.

Do not use this product if you have a known allergy or sensitivity to almonds or any ingredient listed on the product label.

Essential Oil Sensitivity

This product contains orange, pine, mint, and cinnamon essential oils.

Botanical ingredients and essential oils may cause irritation or an allergic reaction in some individuals.

Stop using the product immediately if discomfort or irritation occurs.

Keep Away From Eyes

Avoid direct contact with the eyes.

If the balm enters the eyes, rinse carefully with plenty of clean water. Seek medical attention if irritation continues.

Children and Pets

Keep the balm and its container out of the reach of children and pets.

Children should use the product only under the supervision of a responsible adult.

Pregnancy and Medical Concerns

Speak with a qualified healthcare professional before use if you are pregnant, nursing, receiving medical treatment, or have a serious skin condition or known sensitivity to essential oils.

Storage

Store the tin closed in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight, excessive heat, and moisture.

Because this balm is made with natural oils, changes in temperature may make it softer or firmer. This does not necessarily indicate a problem with the product.

When to Stop Using It

Stop use immediately if you experience burning, persistent redness, swelling, rash, hives, difficulty breathing, or any unexpected reaction.

Seek prompt medical attention for a severe reaction or difficulty breathing.

Important Product Notice

Abundance Balm is a cosmetic product. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or medical condition.

Individual experiences may vary.
