A story of kindness

✦

More Than a Balm

Years ago, during a brutally hot day at Nelson Ledges, a stranger noticed that I was struggling and handed me a small tin of Abundance Balm.

That simple act of kindness stayed with me. After years of searching for the woman who shared it, I began recreating the balm using the ingredients printed on the original tin.

Today, Abundance Balm continues that story—one small act of kindness passed from one person to another.
