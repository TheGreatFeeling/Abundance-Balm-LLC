A story of kindness

✦

More Than a Balm

Years ago, during a brutally hot day at Nelson Ledges, a stranger noticed that Joshua was struggling and handed him a small tin of Abundance Balm.

That simple act of kindness stayed with him. After years of searching for the woman who shared it, he began recreating the balm using the ingredients printed on the original tin.

Today, Abundance Balm continues that story—one small act of kindness passed from one person to another.
