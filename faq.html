Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Abundance Balm.

What is Abundance Balm?

Abundance Balm is a handcrafted botanical balm made with coconut oil, sweet almond oil, jojoba oil, and a blend of carefully selected essential oils. It was inspired by a simple act of kindness that happened years ago at Nelson Ledges.

What can I use it for?

Many customers enjoy using Abundance Balm on dry lips, areas prone to chafing, and as a light botanical fragrance. Individual experiences vary, and the balm is intended for cosmetic use only.

Is it made by hand?

Yes. Every batch is handcrafted with care in small quantities to help maintain quality and consistency.

Does it contain artificial fragrances?

No. The scent comes from the natural essential oils used in the formula.

Is it safe for sensitive skin?

Everyone's skin is different. We recommend performing a patch test before regular use, especially if you have sensitive skin or known allergies.

Does it contain nuts?

Yes. Abundance Balm contains sweet almond oil. If you have a tree nut allergy, please consult your healthcare provider before using products containing almond-derived ingredients.

How should I store it?

Store your balm in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Because it contains natural oils, the texture may become softer in warm temperatures and firmer in cooler temperatures.
